A Shared Vision

Designed by nature.
Refined by Invilla.

The luminary architects at Invilla have crafted the facade with gentle curves forming a wave-like structure — prioritising cross ventilation, natural light, and expansive ocean views. Each level incorporates screening and gardens, while extensive glass expanses on the eastern side frame the sea.

"With soft curves in a wave formation, this understated design focuses on light and views."

— Michael Johnston, Director / Architect, Invilla

Curated Design

Every detail, considered.

Born out of exceptional craftsmanship, the custom details and tailored features are brought to life by a curated selection of natural finishes that harmonise — complementing without overpowering.

Living

Open-plan spaces framed by soaring ocean vistas, with an electric fireplace and seamless indoor-outdoor connection.

Kitchen

Master Suite

Private Rooftop

Sunshine Beach

Nestled against the northern headland.

Sunshine Beach offers a serene oasis shielded from the elements. Relish morning strolls, beachside yoga sessions, and enchanting evenings watching the sunset. Unrivalled access to Noosa's national parklands through to the glistening waters of Main Beach.
